What is Vulnerability Scanning?

Vulnerability scanning is the process of systematically scanning a network or application for security weaknesses, misconfigurations, and vulnerabilities. It helps organizations identify and prioritize potential security risks, allowing them to proactively address these issues before they can be exploited by attackers.
